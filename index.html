Research Vision

Many people still hesitate to seek healthcare due to barriers such as social stigma, financial constraints, and limited access, particularly in rural areas. My long-term goal is to address these challenges by developing machine learning-driven digital health systems that improve accessibility and equity in healthcare.

I am particularly interested in how AI can be applied to mental health, especially in detecting mental health states from real-world data and providing personalized interventions. For example, I hope to explore how AI-based models can support individuals experiencing depression by enabling early detection and tailored, timely interventions.
